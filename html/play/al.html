    • Architecture Lecture Series

    A large-format, multi-fold program/poster announcing SAIC’s School of Architecture Lecture Series for Fall 2024. The design required a careful composition to establish a clear visual hierarchy, guiding the reader through the sequence and meaning of the content. Drawing inspiration from the architects’ projects, I explored how texture—interpreted through fine art techniques like watercolor—can coexist with typography in a way that feels both dynamic and refined while maintaining a serious, professional tone.
