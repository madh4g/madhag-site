    • VCD Publication 2025

    This publication celebrates the curious, experimental nature of VCD students. An accumulation of small-scale experimentation—like shifting a single word half an inch—shapes the concept and identity of a designer's work.

    The VISCOM department instills the confidence and skills to take risks. I set out to craft a publication that embodies this philosophy, expressing it through the most fundamental elements of design—typography and color. Through subtle adjustments and experimentation, the publication reflects the balance between disorder and structure, capturing the refined creative energy that defines each graduating designer.
