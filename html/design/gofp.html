    A hand-bound publication of analogue collage, typography, fine art, and found objects to illustrate the story, "The Garden of Forking Paths" by Jorge Luis Borges.

    Artifacts

    A curated collection of physical and digital artifacts, all meticulously crafted from scratch to visually drive the narrative. Each piece—built through analog collage, hand-rendered typography, original watercolor paintings, and carefully selected found objects—acts as a layered extension of the story itself.
